# Bless

Bless is a basic spell that belongs to the [Water School of Magic](school_of_water_magic.md) and comes with the [Core Game](../content.md).


## Effect

:instant: The selected :unit_ground: or :unit_flying: unit:<br><br>:empower: 0 - Ignores the [Attack die](../dice.md#attack-die) roll.<br>:empower: 1 - Ignores the [Attack die](../dice.md#attack-die) roll and gains +1 :attack:<br>:empower: 2 - Ignores the [Attack die](../dice.md#attack-die) roll and gains +2 :attack:
