# Ammo Cart

Ammo Cart is a war machine that comes with the [Rampart Expansion](../content.md).


## Effect

:permanent: Your :unit_ranged: units ignore combat penalties and gain "+2 :initiative:".


## Cost at Blacksmith

5 :gold:


## Cost at Trade Post

8 :gold:
