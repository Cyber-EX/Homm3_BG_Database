# Wisdom

Wisdom is an ability that comes with the [Core Game](../content.md).

| :---: | :---: |
| Effect | :instant: The cost of buying [spells](spells.md) in this Town is reduced by 2 :gold:. When buying [spells](spells.md) from your Magic Guild, do **Search(3)** instead of **Search(2)**. |
| Expert Effect | :instant: The cost of buying [spells](spells.md) in this Town is reduced by 2 :gold:. When buying [spells](spells.md) from your Magic Guild, do **Search(4)** instead of **Search(2)**. |
